# Crowdforce
Crowdforce is a web app that helps communities take care of their gardens in a systematic way. Crowdforce helps bring order to the gardening schedule and creates opportunities for everyone to contribute to a common cause. The chatbot notifies all community members of tasks that are in the garden. So that everyone can find something to do at a time that suits them.
